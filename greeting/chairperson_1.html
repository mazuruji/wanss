Associate Professor Dr. Tassana Boontong Chairperson, World Academy of Nursing Science (2024-2025)


Associate Professor Dr. Tassana Boontong has been elected as the Chairperson of the World Academy of Nursing Science (WANS) for a second term, and we extend our warmest congratulations on her continued leadership.


WANS is committed to connecting nurse scientists, scholars, and practitioners worldwide to share knowledge and expertise through its biannual academic and research conferences, contributing to meaningful scientific advancement. Building on the strong foundation established during her previous term, Dr. Tassana will continue to advance WANS's mission of fostering academic excellence, strengthening global research collaboration, and expanding engagement with nursing organizations that share the same vision.


Her second term will emphasize promoting evidence generation and innovation to support nursing education, leadership, and service, as well as contributing to global policy directions aligned with the Global Nursing and Midwifery Strategic Directions (2021-2025) and the Global Strategy on Human Resources for Health (2020-2030). WANS looks forward to continued commitment to enhancing research networks, collaboration, and mutual growth within the global nursing science community.



Regards,
Associate Professor Dr. Tassana Boontong
Chairperson, World Academy of Nursing Science
